We would like to extend a huge congratulations to Vivian Schmotzer. Her video entry was selected as the winner of the WasteLAB 2016-2107 High School Viral Video contest. WasteLAB is Greater Greenville Sanitation's mobile and interactive experience designed to advance sustainability through education. Vivian’s video, which you can watch via the link below concerns clothing waste and how it can be curved. Vivian and others submitted their entry to the contest for extra credit towards their recent project in their Environmental Studies class related to impacting waste.
